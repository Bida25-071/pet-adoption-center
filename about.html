About Wame's Cuddle Crew

Wame's Cuddle Crew is a friendly pet adoption center dedicated to helping animals find loving homes. We believe every pet deserves love, care, and a place to call home.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, caring envirinment for pets while connecting them with responsibile, loving families.

Our Volunteers

Our amazing volunteers make everything possible - from feeding and caring for pets to helping new pet owners settle in. We love our team and the work they do!

Our Values

  • Compassion for all animals
  • Community engagement
  • Resonsibility and care
  • Transparency and trust
