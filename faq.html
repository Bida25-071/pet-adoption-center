Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I adopt a pet?

A: Visit our Adoption page(or contact us) to see available pets and submitt an adoption request.

Q: Can I volunteer even if i don't have experience?

A:Absolutely! Our volunteers are trained and guided. Any love for animals helps!

Q: What are your operating hours?

A: We're open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM.

Q: Do you accept donations?

A: Yes! Donations help us feed, care for, and shelter our pets. Contact us for details.

Q: How much does it cost to adopt a pet?

A: Our adoption fee is typically between BWP 150-300, depending on the type of pet.
This fee helps cover vaccinations, medical care, and food.

Q: What does the adoption fee include?

A: The adoption fee usually includes basic vaccinations, deworming,
and a starter pack of food and supplies for your new pet.

Q: Can I adopt if I cannot pay the full fee?

A: We want pets to find loving homes! In some cases, we may offer payment plans or reduced fees — please contact us to discuss options.

Q: Is the adoption fee refundable?

A: No, the adoption fee is not refundable.
It goes directly towards the care of the pets at Wame's Cuddle Crew.
